Sonarbrush

Using the sound cues of 1980s arcade games to help children learn to brush their teeth

Sonarbrush

Prototype with accelerometer attached to head, and computation and wireless signaling on a soldered board.

Circles good - Straight bad

According to dentists, you should brush in circular motions. The Sonarbrush detects this and plays a positive sound when done correctly.

Wiring

Wiring of the soldered stripboard attached to the brush.

Video - Demonstration
